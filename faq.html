Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I open an account online?

    Yes, you can open an account online by filling out the account opening form on our website.

  • What documents do I need to open an account?

    You will need your proof of identity, address proof, and a photograph.

  • How long does it take to open an account?

    The account opening process usually takes 2-3 business days.
